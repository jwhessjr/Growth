This moddule is designed to screen the Russell 2000 stocks to identify growth stocks

A growth stock is defined as one the
1.  Displays a compound average growth rate that is greater than a hurdle rate equal to the sum of the Risk Free Rate (10 year US Treasury Bond) + Equity Risk Premium.
    a.  Revenus last 10 years and last 5 years
    b.  Earnings last 10 years and last 5 years
    c.  Hopefully the growth rate for the last 5 years exceeds the growth rate of the last 10 yeras (growth accellerating)
2.  no Negative EPS over the last 20 quartrs
3.  Debt to equit ratio of < 50% The equity owners control the maority of the company
4.  Has a reasonable P/E ration (<= the average P/E of the S&P 500 (about 19.7 since 1957)

In addition, as with all stock selections the company should be a wealth creator -- ROIC > WACC
